About Me

I decided to redo my portfolio in 2024.

And I decided it would be good to redo and redo it showing the evolution I had during the years I started my journey as a developer.

I started in 2018 doing small basic FrontEnd projects with HTML and CSS, over time I introduced JavaScript. In 2020 I started taking the Technician course in Systems Analysis and Development, at UNOPAR.

And in November 2020, I got an internship opportunity; That's where my journey as a FullStack PHP Developer began. The vacancy came from Teknisa, a company that offers management support solutions to industries in different sectors. Very designed for an order management system in bars and restaurants, with the function of automating all internal processes; In this systems I started working on legacy PHP, with Angular on the Front and SQLServer as the database.

In the year 21, I finished my technical course and was hired, starting as a Junior Developer. In the year 23, our team incorporated another team and I changed projects, now I'm part of a retail website; a modular and integrated solution for commercial automation with numerous customizable benefits.

In June 23, I started my Ethical Hacking and CyberSecurity postgraduate course at VINCIT.

In 22 and 233, I was an instructor in Git and GitHub classes, in an internal Teknisa project, Internship Program, designed to give students the opportunity to take a free mini course and, of course, looking for new talents for the company.
